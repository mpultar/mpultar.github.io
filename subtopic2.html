Past History of AI
Site Author
 Tableau


Abstract
 This website focuses on the past history of AI, and how we got to where we are today. They define Artificial Intelligence, and begin with the creation of AI from 1950-1956. The next section is about the growth of AI between the years of 1957-1979, this was a time of many AI inventions. 1980-1987 is the next period of time written about, these were highly successful years of AI research. This site then goes on to talk about the period of time in which the research and funding of Artificial Intelligence slows down from 1987-1993. From 1993-2011 they list the programs run by Artificial Intelligence invented during that time such as Roomba and Siri. To end the article they briefly touch on the future of Artificial Intelligence.

 The Future of AI
Site Author
 Mike Thomas
Abstract
 This site runs through what the future of Artificial Intelligence looks like. They lay the groundwork with the evolution of AI, and move to the effects Artificial Intelligence will have on the future. The benefits that AI will have on how businesses run more efficiently are explained. The question of job security with the increase of AI is covered. Issues like data privacy, climate change, and the need for regulation are covered. The site then moves on to the Industries and jobs that will be the most affected by the use of AI. Lastly the cons of Artificial Intelligence are discussed thoroughly.
