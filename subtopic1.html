Machine Learning vs Deep Learning
Site Author
 Coursera


Abstract
 This website describes the difference between Machine Learning and Deep Learning in relation to Artificial Intelligence. This article first describes how Artificial Intelligence contains both machine and deep learning. They summarize the differences between the three of them. They go on to explain in further detail how Artificial Intelligence is a "problem solving" program for machines. Next machine learning is explained to be when a machine adapts from tasks its previously performed and improves. Deep learning is then said to mimic the human brain in the way that it processes data. Within each section they give you examples of a program that was created with each subset.

 Weak AI vs Strong AI
Site Author
 Ellen Glover
Abstract
 This website presents us with the two different kinds of Artificial Intelligence. It begins with what is considered to be "Artificial Intelligence". The definition of Weak AI is presented, alternate names such as "Narrow AI" are utilized for Weak AI. They go over the types of AI that are considered to be a type of Weak AI. Examples of Weak AI are given. Strong AI is the next topic spoken about. Strong AI gets defined and its other name Artificial general intelligence is stated. It goes on to talk about how Strong AI works and evolves. They give examples from pop culture of what would be considered Strong AI, if it existed yet. The pros and cons of Strong AI are stated. Superintelligence is introduced and the concept is explained. They speak about how it will be known when AI has finally reached the point of superintelligence.
